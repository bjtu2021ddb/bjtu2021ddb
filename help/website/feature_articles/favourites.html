Using Favorites for Android

In OsmAnd, you can add places to Favorites to save their location and make navigation even handier. To add a place to favorites, just make a long tap on the map, then choose Add to favorites (Star icon) or GPX Waypoint. After that, you'll be able to find it in your Favorites list in seconds.

Favorites and GPX Waypoint: when you edit or create a new point you can choose colors, icons, and shapes for it. You can create groups of Favorites by giving them a certain name in the Category submenu as you add them. Adding a name and a description to a new Favorite location is possible, as well.

View Favorites on the map for Android

To view all your favorites on the map, just go to the dashboard, and tap Show all on the Favorites menu, then tap option button and choose Show on map. You can also choose the color of the group. This works just fine with separate groups of favorites.

The other way to view your favorites is just go to Configure map - Show - Favorites, remember about individual settings for each navigation profile. You'll see all your favorites on the map.

You can easily choose the favorite points to be your waypoints in a trip: just use the waypoint button (Actions-> Add GXP waypoint) and add those you need to the trip. Tap the button once again.

To delete a favorite or group of favorites, just hit the trashbin button, then check them and press delete in the Favorites list.

Using Favorites for iOS

In OsmAnd for iOS, you can add places to Favorites to save their location and make navigation even handier. To add a place to favorites, just make a long tap on the map, then choose Add favorite. After that, you'll be able to find it in your Favorites list in seconds. You can create groups of Favorites by giving them a certain name in the Category submenu as you add them. Adding a name, color and a description to a new Favorite location is possible, as well.

View Favorites on the map for iOS

To view all your favorites on the map go to menu Map - Show on Map - Favorite - Turn On. You'll see all your favorites on the map.

You can export favorite points as files from your device and share it with another device. To export/import favorite, go to menu My Places > Favorites > Import/Export.

To add favorite as your waypoint, go to menu My Places > Favorite > Select needed point > Press the Actions-> Add waypoint.

To create a trip with favorite points, enter the My Places menu > Trips, choose Create new trip and then add favorite spots and description. Then hit Save. You'll be able to view your saved trip in My places menu > Trips.
